\section{Introduction}
Let ${}^{w}P_{l}$ be the pose of the lidar in the world coordinate system. This matrix maps points from the lidar coordinate system to the world. The same for the camera, with ${}^{w}P_{c}$ the pose of the camera in the world coordinate system.

\bigskip
You are looking for the transformation which maps points from the lidar coordinate system to the camera coordinate system. So, you just need to compose them correctly:

${}^{c}P_{l} = {}^{c}P_{w} \; {}^{w}P_{l} = ({}^{w}P_{c})^{-1} \; {}^{w}P_{l} $

\bigskip
With this matrix you can then transform lidar points to the camera coordinates system:
\begin{equation}
X_{camera} = {}^{c}P_{l}\;X_{lidar} = {}^{c}P_{l}\;
 \left[ 
 \begin{array}{c}
           x \\
           y \\
           z \\
           1
         \end{array}
         \right]_{lidar}
\end{equation}
